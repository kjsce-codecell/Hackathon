About KJSCE Hackathon
The KJSCE hackathon 2017 saw the particiapation of all tech-savy people across Mumbai and we were in awe of the innovation ideas and brilliant products in display. The dedicated approach by the participants helped set a high standard for the comprition in its first year itself. Hackathon 2017 was held in KJSCE as a part of the annual technical fest, Abhiayntriki. This tradition will be followed and we will be hosting the Hackathon 2018 during Abhiyantriki 2018, a two day festival from 5th October to 6th October.

FAQ
What happens at a Hackathon?
Participants (“hackers”) spend 36 hours working in teams of 1 - 5 people to build or code or design projects (“hacks”) that they’re excited about. There are workshops, mentors, food, swag, and buckets of coffee to guide you along the way. You bring your ideas, and we give you everything you need to make them come to life.
What shoud I bring?
You should bring your student ID and anything you’d need for a sleepover: laptop, charger, deodorant (!), change of clothes, etc.. You won’t need food or drinks; we’ve got you covered there.
What do people normally make?
Hacks generally fall under one (or more) of the seven cortices: Web Development, Android, iOS, AI/ML, AR/VR, Game Development, and Hardware.
What should I not bring?
Weapons of any kind, drugs, or alcohol. If you’re not sure whether something will be okay, please ask ahead of time!
What are the topics and the prizes for the Hackathon?
The topic and the prizes will be declared soon.So keep an eye on the KJSCE-CodeCell Facebook Page. Also once you've registered with an e-mail id , we'll shoot you a mail about further announcements
What if i don't have a team or an idea?
Not to worry, most people don’t! We have some great activities planned before and during the event to help you meet teammates and start thinking through your ideas.
Do you allow walk-ins?
We will not be allowing walk-ins. To ensure that you’ll be allowed in, please apply ahead of time.
Did we just miss something..
You can just drop a DM at any of our social network handles or gitter and we'll try our best to answer them ASAP ;)
